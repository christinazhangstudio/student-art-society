ABOUT

Student Art Society is a registered student organization at the University of Texas at San Antonio. SAS provides a network and space for those who enjoy the arts, providing opportunities to enhance aesthetic appreciation, engage with others, and learn art principles. The club offers students with academically informed, hands-on instruction in the practice of a variety of visual media. Some of our activities include:

  • Art Workshops
  • Art-themed Socials
  • Plein Air Sessions
  • Art History & Culture Discussions
  • Field trips & Outings
  • UTSA Events (Dia en la Sombrilla, BestFest, etc.)
